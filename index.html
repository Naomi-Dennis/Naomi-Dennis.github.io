About Me

Welcome to my page!

My name is Naomi Dennis. I am a full stack web developer, specializing in back end technologies such as nodejs and rails. I have had a love of technology from a young age. Introduced to the world of programming by my hobbyist father, I built my first web page at the age of 9. In middle school, I joined the robotics team as team leader before settling into a role of lead programmer. I participated in small regional competitions before my first large competition at Georgia's B.E.S.T Robotics Competition in 2011. There, my team earned a design award. Later that year I participated in the FIRST robotics competition, where I headed my team in creating an interface with an XBOX KINECT.
